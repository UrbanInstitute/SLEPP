Usage Instructions

  • To change the grading criteria for a given variable, click on the "(Change Criteria)" button beneath the variable, which will show the current intervals (or fixed value) used in calculating the grade.
  • Interval notation is as follows: "[" and "]" represent left and right Inclusive intervals, while "(" and ")" represent Exclusive intervals. Brackets of both types can be mixed, giving half inclusive/exclusive intervals.
  • Once the new critera are set, click "Calculate" to re-calculate all the grades (including "Overall"). Click "Defaults" to reset the plan criteria to their defaults.
  • You can change the criteria of one grade, and re-calculate, while viewing another grade. This lets you see changes to "Overall" upon changing individual grade criteria.
